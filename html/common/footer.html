Additional Information

Information, Communication and Technology Laboratory (ICTLab) is a joint international ICT research laboratory between USTH and Vietnamese/French partners. It involves researchers coming from USTH, IOIT (Institute of Information Technology, Hanoï), IRD (Institut de Recherche pour le Développement) and the University of La Rochelle, France.

ICTLab was created on December 1st, 2014. Its creation was supported by USTH, the French Embassy in Vietnam, 13 French high education institutes and universities (USTH Consortium), and the ADB (Asian Development Bank).

Contact

ICTLab is located in the heart of the VAST (Vietnamese Academy of Science and Technology) complex, in the city center of Hanoï, Vietnam.
